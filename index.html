Rainbow Square

Snap App

I created a snap project with a variety of different variables to make a series or squares in a certain perspective. Our goal was to just make a block to draw a square and to be able to change the side length very easily and we did it by making loops but I decided to take it a step further and I wanted to make random sized squares. The challenging part was putting it into perspective but I figured out that if you do a little math to distance the square based on its random side length you could then have the random square make themself into perspective.
